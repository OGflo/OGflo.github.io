About

                                        SUPPORT

When you choose to design to style with our website you get a one on one experiance and help with your order placement and arrival . also to not confuse us we will contact you at from 7am-5pm any day of the week (these are also the hours for support help)

                                        PICK UP

When you place an order we try and communicate so that we can make your pickup as soon as you want your clothing item to be picked up. That can be as soon or as late as you want with a maximum wait time of 4 weeks, I will then delete your information and continue to the next personafter that wait time.

                                        DILIVERY

when your order has been complete we will to 1 of 3 things to notify you we will either 1 text/call you 2 email you or 3(coming soon) notifiy you via our app then you will be notified of your dilivery persons and will be given your clothing back with your design
